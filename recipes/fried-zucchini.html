Fried Zucchini

Description

Crispy Fried Zucchini is a delicious way to enjoy all of that summer zucchini from your yardâ€¦or your well-meaning neighbor or coworker. Zucchini is sliced into medallions, coated in a parmesan breadcrumb mixture and fried until crispy!

Ingredients

  • 3 medium zucchini
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 3/4 cup cornmeal
  • 3/4 cup cornmeal
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper

Steps

  1. Cut zucchini into 1/8-in. thick coins. Place eggs in one shallow bowl and cornmeal in another; dip zucchini into the egg and then coat with cornmeal.
  2. Heat oil in a large nonstick skillet over medium heat. In batches, place zucchini in oil. Fry over medium heat until lightly browned, 1-2 minutes. Flip each piece, fry until golden brown, 1-2 minutes longer.
  3. Sprinkle hot zucchini with salt and pepper. Serve immediately.
