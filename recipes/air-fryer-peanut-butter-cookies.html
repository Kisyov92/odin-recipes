Air-Fryer Peanut Butter Cookies

Description

An air fryer can bake up a few cookies in about half the time an oven takes, and it doesn't need much time for preheating. Plus, you'll still get the same texture and flavor you're looking for!

Ingredients

  • 1 large egg, room temperature, beaten
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup creamy peanut butter

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, mix all ingredients. Roll level tablespoons of dough into balls. Flatten with a fork.
  2. Preheat air fryer to 400Â°. In batches, place 1 in. apart in greased air fryer. Cook until lightly browned, 3-4 minutes. Allow to cool slightly on the pan and remove to wire racks to cool.
